Website project for watchmaker and locksmith shop (Bechelor Degree Thesis)

INTRODUCTION

The Bachelor thesis has implementation character. It describes graphic interface of website made as prototype. Website’s goal is to facilitate interaction of service provider and client during realisation of material services at ”Watchmaker’s and Locksmith’s Shop WALDI”. The prototype was built on the basis of the User Experience Design process. The process consists of 5 stages: Product Definition, Research, Analysis, Prototype Building and Testing. The design work was started from an interview with the owner of the shop, who also participated in further work on the project. The project paid special attention to the needs of users, which were examined at the initial stage of design during research stage. On the basis of the resulting conclusions from collected data the prototype has been designed. Created solution was tested by potential users and updated until the tests were successfully completed by all participants.

I Phase -- Product definition

Project has been started from product definition phase which goal was to get to know the sense of creating the solution. This concept is the basis for final product. The phase includes an interview with shop owner.
The interview includes five thematic blocks:

Project background
The service activity of the "WALDI" company was started in 2006 and was carried out on nearby marketplaces. The shop in shopping mall was opned in 2013.
Shop "WALDI" is located in "Revia Park" shopping mall in Zamość. In average month shop is visited by about 300 independent people and most of them are not more frequent than once a month. The shopping mall is located on the outskirts of the city and is not a very popular place.
The shopping mall is fully opened 9:00 am, but the "WALDI" service point due to having one cash register and conducting business also at marketplaces, officially opens at 1:00 p.m. and closes at 8:00 p.m.. Work at marketplaces depends on many factors, such as the weather, so sometimes the shop is opened from 9:00 a.m..
Departures to the marketplaces are characterized by the need to start work at 5 a.m. -- therefore, after returning from work, the owner needs a two-hour break, also in the event of illness or for other private reasons, the shop is run by the spouse owner, or one of his sons, who cannot provide all services provided at the "WALDI" service point. Work in the markerplaces, causes that the services can be provided also in: Józefów, Łaszczów, Tyszowce and Zakrzówek.
The owner, when asked about the problems he faces on a daily basis at work, stated that that there are basically two main problems, one of them is the inability to implement some services ordered by clients (this applies to the lack of a suitable machine) and the other specified by the owner as follows: "Clients sometimes come with services that my sons and wife are unable to do, things such as coding the immobilizer or repairing the watch, the clients often leave the shop and do not come back. " - this generally applies to things that customers can't leave at the shop -- like the only key to the car or they live far away from the shopping mall and it won't pay off.

Aim of the project
The owner of the service point is not familiar with the possibilities offered by the Internet for his company. He would like the website to meet the needs of customers, advertise the service point and improve communication with customers.

Target group
The target group for the solution are people who are from 20 to 45 years old, because according to the GUS's research (Central Statistical Office) these are the people who use the internet the most ofte -- 95% of people living in Poland in age from 16 to 45 use internet, older ones use internet less often. As the company provides locksmith services the target group has been narrowed down to age range starting from 20 years old.

Competition
Competition for "WALDI" company is located around Zamość, as the service point provides different services, the comptetition has been devided into three groups:
- watchmakers,
- locksmiths,
- trade.

Project scope
At this phase of work on the project, expectations for the solution are general.
Owner stated that:
- there must be "the scope of provided services, the saled ware and the hourly work schedule"
- there may be a "system for communication with the clients and old clocks for sale”.

II Phase -- Research

The purpose of the research phase is to find out about real or potential needs and problems of clients that have an impact on their choices and behavior. Tools used to achieve these goals are: empathy maps, ethnographic interviews, user observations, exploratory surveys, analysis of the environment and needs in the context of functionality. Due to the limitations of face to face contacts resulting from the COVID-19 pandemic, the activities that were carried out during the project include exploratory surveys among current customers of the "WALDI" service point and the observation of service providing in the shop.

Exploratory Surveys
The survey consists of 17 questions. The goals of survey are to get to know:
- customer motivation to use the service point,
- customer communication needs,
- effective ways to advertise the company.
Motivation
The "WALDI" service point can be found via website of shopping mall Revia Park, or via Google Maps. However, inferring from customers' statements few of them gets to the shop, because of the above solutions:
Most people came to the shop for the first time, because they were passing by (38.1%) or thanks to a friend (33.3%). Looking at the indicated level of satisfaction with the services provided in the shop "WALDI" it can also be stated that the customers are satisfied with the provided services.
The motivation that made the respondents of the survey and people being in the shop during observation use the services from "WALDI" shop are:
- an apartment close to the shopping mall Revia Park,
- friendly relations,
- honesty,
- well-performed services,
- good prices.

The scope of provided services
The answers provided in the survey indicate that clients don't know the entire scope of the realized services provided by the company "WALDI". This may be due to the lack of need in provoding these services, but at the time when such a service will be needed customers still won't know that they can do it there.

Information provided about provided service
Some of the respondents indicated the reasons why they are not "very satisfied" with the provided informations about the provided services:
- knowing the price of the service only after its completion,
- getting to know that the service they came with can't be provided at this time or can't be provided at all,
- waiting.

Possibility to contact with "WALDI" Shop
There are two ways to contact the "WALDI" service point, this is physical contact in the shop or telephone contact. The phone isn't always answered on the first contact attempt and sometimes due to poor coverage in the shopping mall missed calls arrive much later than they should, therefore some of the respondents pointed to problems with the possibility of contacting the service company "WALDI".

Considering "WALDI" Shop as the best choice in respondents case
Most of the respondents were determined to recognize the "WALDI" service shop as the best choice in their case, because:
- the point has an appropriate location,
- the owner of the point is honest,
- customers have not encountered better prices,
- customers had a problem with competing companies.

Additional remarks
In this part of the survey, the respondents expressed their will to have information on whether the owner is currently in the service shop and a greater range of possibilities of contacting the shop.

Comment to the survey
The surveys were distributed among the current customers of service shop. The questionnaire was available both on paper and in the form of a link on a sheet of paper (with the code QR). Responses were obtained from 21 people diversified in terms of age, gender and frequency of using the shop's services. Answers included in the questionnaire on paper were entered into the online spreadsheet.
Observations
The observation included two sessions divided into:
- owner service - two hours,
- service by the owner's son - two hours.
The observation lasted for four hours in total and included:
- conversations during and after the services were provided,
- duration of individual services,
- refusal to provide the service (due to the lack of the owner, tools, skills),
- traffic intensity in the shopping mall,
- general conclusions that emerged during the observation.

IV Phase -- Design

Information Architecture
The website has been devided into nine subpages. The sections called "The owner is / is not currently in the Revia Park Gallery" and the "basket of products" will be accessible from any subpage of the website.
During the development of the information architecture, one of the ideas was to implement the possibility of booking time for the service, but due to the way the company works, it would n't be possible. People waiting in the queue can't be served after the customer who appeared at the shop a moment ago - there is a high probability that customers from the queue would resign from providing services at the "WALDI" service point, in addition in such a case, people who don't use internet would be aggrieved.
Initially, the owner did't want to disclose the prices of the services due to competition, but because of the benefits presented in connection with their availability (the prices are attractive to customers), he changed his mind.
The number of possible services at "WALDI" company is large, therefore, if all services were on one page it could bewould be problematic to find the service. The "Implemented Services" subpage allows users to find out the price (or price range), instructions, hours in which the service can be provided and the time needed for it's implementation after going through a maximum of three questions narrowing the scope of services in the circle of User's interests. Online sales carried out by a service company is limited to the sale of old mechanical clocks. Online Trading with the remaining goods would force a number of changes in the company, which at the moment is not possible.
The possibility of logging in will include only the owner's account, that's why the registration option wasn't presented in the information architecture.

V Phase -- Validation

The number of tasks results from the time in which the users are able to perform them, i.e. in about 15 minutes.The test tasks concerned the main functionalities that end users can use. The tasks in which it was possible had context - the user could imagine himself in a given situation, thus undertaken activities could be closer to the situation in everyday life. The tasks were structured the way that doesn't suggest answers to users, and to not be incomprehensible to them.

User testing - Clients

The test among potential customers of service shop "WALDI" included five respondents who varied in terms of age and gender. Each person participating in the test was between the ages of twenty and forty five, because the solution is addressed to such respondents. Usability tests revealed problems that potential users of the solutions had. Presented below conclusions are the results of testing.

First task: Browse the site for 10-15 seconds and answer the question - What does "WALDI" company do?
The users were able to find out what the page was about within 10-15 seconds. These were general information about the offer of the "WALDI" company. Tests participants reported the services they observed on the home page: making keys, repairing clocks / watches, sharpening. The respondents didn't talk about the assortment for sell.


Task two: You are on tour and you would like to find out:
a) Is the shop in shopping mall opened?
b) Will you be able to make a copy of car key in the Revia Park shopping mall now? It is Thursday 15:30.
During this task, users didn't see the banner at the top of the home page that said "Owner is currently available at the shop", but most of them noticed the "Work schedule" button located on the right side of banner and used it to check if the shop was opened.


Task three: You work near the Revia Park Shopping mall. You have a half hour break and in the meantime, you would like to buy rechargeable batteries.
a) check whether on Thursday between 2:15 pm and 2:30 pm the shop will be open,
b) check whether "WALDI" sells rechargeable batteries.
The content of point a was very similar to the content of the first task, therefore problems didn't appear during this task. In point b the range of assortment for sell was unclear i.e. users could find information about the services provided on the home page, but they couldn't see the assortment for sell - that's why they had to use menu placed in the right corner of the nav bar.

Task four: Next week you go to your uncle's birthday party and you know that he is a fan of mechanical clocks. Try to buy Adler's "Mechanical Pendulum Clock" from 2015 without logging in and by filling in the appropriate fields as follows:
a) Payment method: payment in advance.
b) Delivery method - courier.
c) Method of fiscalisation: invoice.
d) Payment method: bank transfer.
e) Bank: choose any.

During the fourth task, one of the respondents had a problem with finding the button leading to the subpage where he can buy a clock. He started his search from the subpage "Range of sold goods", not "Clocks for sale".

Task five: You have a mechanical clock at home that you would like to repair. You don't know what's wrong. See if the "WALDI" company is able to help you with such case.
This task posed no problems to the study participants.

Task six: You have a large number of sieves and knives for a grinding machine, you know the price of the competitor and you want to see at what price you can get this service in "WALDI" company.
a) Check the price of the above service.
b) Send an e-mail to lower the found price.
This task posed no problems to the study participants.

Task seven: You live in Józefów and you would like to get a copy of key to your car.
a.1) Check if the "WALDI" company operates in Józefów,
a.2) if so, check where you can find them.
b) Check if the attendant in Józefów will be able to perform this service.
At the beginning, each of the tests participants searched for information about the "WALDI" company's location in the "About company" subpage. People who started looking for in "Work Schedule"weren't sure if they are at the right page, they didn't read the description at the top of the page + information about marketplaces is under the work schedule of Revia Park shopping mall.


The participants didn't know where they could find information about availability of the service, which could be found after clicking the information button.


User testing - Owner

The tests, carried out with the owner of the shop, covered five tasks and concerned the available functionalities in the control panel. The tasks were tested on a prototype in mobile and computer version. In both cases, the first task was to log into the control panel.
- Task one: You are currently at the shop and suddenly you have to close it, change the settings to "Shop closed"

- Tast two: Try to add a new service to the system.

- Taskt three: You know that on Tuesday you will be working at the shop from 1:00 pm. Enter a change in the system.

- Taskt four: Try to add a new brand of watches to the system.

- Task five: You don't like the photo thumbnail on the sale offer "Mechanical clock with pendulum"
a) delete the clock picture,
b) set the next picture as the main picture,
c) undo made changes.
During testing, the owner encountered a problem in undoing changes made on the website. The icon representing the button wasn't associated with activity history.


Implemented changes

On the basis of conclusions resulting from usability tests, prototypes has been changed:
- Changed the banner color to a more contrasting with the background color.



- Added "Range of products for sell" on the home page.


- Added information about the places where "WALDI" company works and links to the informationtions specifying the offer of the shop.


- Changed the icon and name of the button leading to the shop - from "Clocks for sale" to "Buy a clock".



- Divided the work schedule subpage into two separate pages: Revia Parkshopping mall and Targowiska.



- Changed the button leading to information about the range of provided services by the attendant (text tells what is hidden under this button).


- Added a signature to the "Activity history" button.


Presentation of solution

General description

The solution was designed in figma. The website design was implemented in two variants: one for the phone screen - 375x815 pixels corresponding to the screen size of the iPhone 11 Pro and the other for the screen of a computer browser in the size of 1350x698 pixels corresponding to the size of full-size browser windows on the computer that was used for testing solutions. The designed mock-ups were inspired by the Material Design system. The website is designed in a flat design style. The project was implemented in the Mobile First approach.

Colors

The colors of the page have been adapted to the colors used by the service facility "WALDI". The colors correspond to the colors used for the company's advertising banners.



In addition to the colors of the company's advertising banners, inspiration was taken from the internet.

Violet color (#4E3189) is a color that contrasts with both the background color and the companies colors - therefore it is the main color used for interactions on the page. Color pink (#EE3D55) was used to interact on white (#FFFFFF), or gray (# F4F0ED) backgroud and served for secondary interactions, e.g. to remove items from the shopping basket.


Text written on a white (#FFFFFF) or gray (#F4F0ED) background appeared in black (#000000) color and text written on a different color background is in white (#FFFFFF) color. The background color of the project is gray (#F4F0ED) while the white color (#FFFFFF) was used as background color of the section on the website, because of the difference between gray (#F4F0ED) and white (#FFFFFF), you can distinguish individual sections from each other. Muted companie's colors were also used as background colors, for: the navigation bar, footer, shop section and the contact section. Muted colors of the company along with the emerging wave refer to the characteristic elements of the company's advertising banners. The colors have been muted because they are used as elements of the background and shouldn't focus users attention.



Icons

The icons used in the project come from a variety of sources, some of them were: made for project needs, reworked from the existing icons, used without any changes.



Subpages

Provided services
The website allows users to check the details of the implementation services.The number of services provided by "WALDI" is quite extensive, therefore they have been sorted. The user is able to find the service by clicking on the appropriate buttons, which will narrow down the scope of services to single examples.Information about services include: its availability at a given moment, time for implementation, description (if added) and the price range.For other information, users can contct with the owner. Services not described on the page can also be discussed by phone or e-mail.



Range of products for sale
The sub-page "Range of products for sale" presents the products available in the service company "WALDI", it's appearance resembles the sub-page "Services provided". Subpage includes brands, product types and description (if added).



Buy a clock
The "Buy a clock" subpage allows you to buy a clock online or view the available clocks at the moment. The buying process is limited to three steps, which are: "Select delivery and payment", "Fill in the necessary details", "Pay", the steps that have been completed are marked with the icon and their transparency is increased by 30%. Step, which the user has not yet reached has a white background, the stage where the user is currently in has no transparency and a purple color. The owner of the company decided that the only sold products on the website will be clocks.



Work schedule
The "Work schedule" subpage presents information about the opening hours of "WALDI" service points, their location, the attendant and the scope of services available at a specific time. It is divided into a service facility in Revia Park shopping mall and facilities at marketplaces.



Contact us
The website presents two methods of contact. One of them is telephone contact and the other is an e-mail contact, to which can be used the form available on the page.



About company
The "About company" subpage contains links to: work schedule, services provided, scope of sold productsand a description of the company.



Control panel
The "Control Panel" subpage is available only to the owner and after logging in. Registration isn't possible on the website, because at a given stage of the company's development accounts will be used by owner to make changes in the website. The panel allows to make changes that are considered as frequent and is intended to enable the owner to update the site himslef. The available functions are divided into two sections: "Time and place of work", "Products and services".The changes are saved automatically.





The user has the option to withdraw the made changes within 24 hours in the history of changes.
