Improving the quality of the visit at Hospital Emergency Ward

ABOUT THE PROJECT

As project subject we have chosen the work of Hospital Emergency Ward. The whole process of designing have been done by Service Design Method. This subject has been chosen because of noticeable need of improvement in this area. Hospital Emergency Ward is the place where people come when they are in need of sudden intervention. That’s why all conveniences , speeding up waiting on visit are needed, so that’s what our goals in this project were.

MADE: AS UNIVERSITY PROJECT
FINISHED: 01.2020

DESIGNING PROCESS

Division of design works

As a team we have together gone through the design process. The part that we devided was: interviews, observations, testing, prototyping.
From written above I was responsible for:
- 1 observation
- designing form prototyp
- 1 test with user

Persona:

People that are using services provided by Hospital Emergency Ward are older and young people, with disabilities and without - so we dedicate our solution to a wide group of recipients.

Empathy:

In the Process of empathy we have made 2 times 2 hours observations in the Hospital Emergency Ward waiting room and 2 interviews ( 1 with an older person and the other with a young one). We presented our results by Story- Sharing Method. In 5 different students groups by World Cafe Method we discussed associations on the topic of Hospitals. Here are the results:

Personnel:
- No decision maker that would take the responsibility.
- No personnel help.
- No discipline among workers.

Waiting:
- “We don’t a thing - just wait”.
- You never know how sudden your case is and when you will go to see the doctor.
- Uncomfortable waiting conditions

Signs:
-” It’s hard to reach the Hospital Emergency Ward at the first time.”
- Patients are not informed enough.

Problem Defining:



- Waiting at the HEW
Our Goal is to decrease queue on each HEW and make waiting more comfortable for patients. We want to pay our attention to patients and people that came with them to hospital - they may pass through a big amount of stress during doctor visit.

- Lack of informations
We would like patients to be prepared on circumstances which appears at HEW. In this way we will limit unnecessary patients stress and people, that had to bring patient’s list of drugs to the doctor.

- Coming at the HEW
We would like to improve orientation on the way to the hospital and during the time people are inside of it to decrease the time spend on asking about the way and searching for doctor’s offices in the hospital.

Ideation:

In the Ideation Process through the methods: Scamper, brain - writing and brain - storm we generated ideas in which by method now, how, wow we grouped up the ideas by level of impact and difficulty in making the idea come to reality, because of that we could filter the ideas that weren’t rationale. So from a big amount of ideas we have chosen 2:

- application form
- interactive map

About solution:

Our solution is the applications having 2 functionalities - interactive map and registration to the HEW visit (We took only instances where the Ambulance wasn’t needed). The registration can be made from any place and any time - you just have to have a smartphone with internet connection. We didn’t want to create registration platform that would be anonime, because then there wouldn't be a possibility to punish people, that didn’t come to the visit. - that’s why registration requires coming to one of HEW with ID card. In case of minor and unregistered many people can register from 1 account. In the hospital people not only comes to visit the doctor. They are often waiting for doctor’s copy and in many cases it takes few hours, because of that we added a mode to give opportunity to doctors to do this through application. To use the application of course patient have to have a smartphone or computer, because of that we tried to make transparent and easy to use interface, so that people, who are not familiar with technology will have less issues with using it. Interactive map would use virtual extension of reality by using phone’s webcam to show the way to different Doctor's Offices.
