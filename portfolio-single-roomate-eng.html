Help in searching for optimal roommate - Roomate

ABOUT THE PROJECT

Due to the age of the recipient group (20-30 years), the solution that I propose is an application that allows users to mainly contact each other.
The proposed name of the application is: Roomate. (Logo proposed below with name)
Short name, easy to remember and related to the subject.br
If the application were only supposed to make contact between different users, why would it be needed in the era of facebook, instagram, snapchat and many other platforms that allow establishing relationships online?
Well, as you might expect, the application is not focused mainly on looking for friends and relationships online, but on looking for roommates, tenants, apartments in an easy and intuitive way.
Below is the description of prototype I created.

MADE: AS EXERCISE
FINISHED: 10.2019

ABOUT PROTOTYPE

The prototype was created in Polish language, which of course does not exclude adding additional language versions of the application.
Basic application functions:
- Search for tenants / roommates
- Flat / room search
- Collecting contacts
- Sending a message
- Creating virtual reflections of real housing communities

DESIGNING PROCESS

Introduction

54% of people are living in the urban communities. Average age for people to take a marriage is for man 29 years (26 years only 2 decades ago) and woman is 27 years old (23 2 decades ago) - because of that people living in the city spends most of their lives between 20 - 30 years old with roommates or flatmates. Finding and staying for a longer time with a good flatmate is becoming harded because more and more people are coming to the cities.

Problem Defining

How to improve searching and choosing the flat/room mates?
